Chef's Favorites

Truffle Arancini

Golden fried risotto balls infused with wild mushrooms and truffle oil, served with a delicate garlic aioli. A perfect starter to awaken your palate.

Wagyu Beef Carpaccio

Thinly sliced premium Wagyu beef, garnished with aged parmesan shavings, fresh rocket, and a drizzle of truffle oil. Melt-in-your-mouth excellence.

Pan-Seared Scallops

Succulent scallops seared to perfection, resting on a smooth cauliflower purée and topped with crispy pancetta. A harmony of textures and flavors.
