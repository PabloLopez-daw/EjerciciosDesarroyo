Our History

Our restaurant was born from a desire to share authentic flavors and meaningful moments. What began as a small family dream grew through a passion for good food and hospitality, serving every guest as if they were home.

With dedication, fresh ingredients, and recipes carrying tradition, we have grown alongside our customers, now part of our extended family.

Culinary Philosophy

At Horizon, cooking is more than technique; it is a tribute to heritage, nature, and emotion.

We source fresh, seasonal ingredients from trusted local producers, turning every dish into the "author of the land". Our recipes honor tradition while embracing creativity.

From the first aromas to the last bite, we create experiences that nourish both body and soul.

The Origin

True flavor begins before the kitchen. We honor the "Author of the Land" by partnering with small-scale artisans who share our respect for nature's cycles.

Every ingredient tells a story of patience and dedication. From the morning dew on our greens to the pristine waters of our coast.

The Cellar

A journey through the world's finest vineyards. Our sommelier has curated a collection that bridges the gap between classic heritage and modern winemaking.

Whether you prefer a robust vintage or a delicate white, our cellar is designed to elevate every nuance of our tasting menu.
