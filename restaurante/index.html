Our History

Our restaurant was born from a desire to share authentic flavors and meaningful moments. What began as a small family dream grew through a passion for good food and hospitality, serving every guest as if they were home.

With dedication, fresh ingredients, and recipes carrying tradition, we have grown alongside our customers, now part of our extended family. Here, every dish celebrates our roots, and every visit is an invitation to a unique experience.

Culinary Philosophy

At Horizon, cooking is more than technique; it is a tribute to heritage, nature, and emotion.

We source fresh, seasonal ingredients from trusted local producers, turning every dish into the "author of the land". Our recipes honor tradition while embracing creativity, blending innovation with timeless flavors.

From the first aromas to the last bite, we create experiences that nourish both body and soul. Every dish is a gesture of care, every detail a reflection of our culinary values: authenticity, respect, and joy.

Special Events

At Horizon, family is at the heart of everything we do.

Over the years, we have had the honor of hosting birthdays, anniversaries, weddings, and moments, each a reflection of love, tradition, and togetherness.

Our warm hospitality and cuisine allow you to share past stories, photo exchanges, and memories that become precise at the table. Whether it's an intimate dinner or a joyful gathering, we craft every detail to make your celebration unforgettable.
